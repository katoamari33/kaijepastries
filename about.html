Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming Fort Portal's favorite bakery

Our History

2018

Humble Beginnings

Kaije Pastries was founded by Mrs Katekaine Shadiah and Mrs Kaboneke Shamiilah (R.I.P) as a small home recipe bakery in Kitumba. The two sisters shared a passion for baking and started supplying neighbors with their delicious treats.

2019

First Commercial Space

Due to increasing demand, the bakery moved to a small commercial space in Nyakasura, Fort Portal Tourism City, allowing them to serve more customers.

2021

Expansion

The bakery expanded its product line and moved to a larger location to accommodate growing production needs and customer base.

Present

Community Favorite

Today, Kaije Pastries is a beloved local institution known for its quality baked goods and commitment to traditional recipes with modern touches.

Our Team
