Welcome to Kaije Pastries

Founded in 2018 as a small home recipe bakery in Kitumba by Mrs Katekaine Shadiah and Mrs Kaboneke Shamiilah (R.I.P), Kaije Pastries has grown into a beloved bakery in Nyakasura, Fort Portal Tourism City. We specialize in creating delicious cakes, daddies, and cookies using traditional recipes with a modern twist.
